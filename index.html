Explainable changes, complete audit trail.

Pharma data is fragmented by design.

Teams buy data from multiple vendors. Internal systems evolve in parallel. Records for providers, organizations, affiliations, and activity rarely match cleanly.

The result: expensive manual reconciliation that delays analytics, reporting, and field execution.

Why existing approaches fail.

  • Dashboards display problems — they do not resolve them.
  • Warehouses store problems — they do not reconcile them.
  • Centralization projects are slow and often unrealistic.
  • Source systems will never fully agree on their own.

What Kernel is.

Kernel is the reconciliation layer across your existing data ecosystem.

  • Runs inside your cloud.
  • Connects to data where it already lives.
  • Detects conflicts and proposes fixes with AI agents.
  • Keeps humans in the loop for verification.
  • Maintains explainability and audit logs for every change.

Kernel keeps pharmaceutical data consistent, explainable, and trustworthy — no matter where it lives.
